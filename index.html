About Us

Our Restaurant

✻

We would like to welcome you to Little Piazza Bar & Grill, your favourite Italian family restaurant in Zetland for almost 10 years. We have a simple philosophy; great food, generous servings and a relaxed atmosphere, just what you expect of your local Italian restaurant.
Our menu has something for everyone; a tasty selection of classic dishes from the grill, appetizing pizzas and pastas and mouth-watering desserts. We also offer most of our menu as take-away and home delivery.
